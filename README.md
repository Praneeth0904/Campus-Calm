## 🧘 CampusCalm – UX/UI Case Study

**Designing a calm and mindful digital experience for better mental well-being.**

A UX/UI case study focused on creating a peaceful, accessible, and user-friendly wellness platform.
Crafted in Figma with an emphasis on **clarity, emotional design, and usability**.

---

## 🌿 Project Overview

CampsCalm is designed to help users manage stress, build healthy habits, and explore mindfulness practices in a simple and engaging way.
The goal was to create a calm, distraction-free experience that encourages consistency and ease of use.

---

## 🧠 The Problem

Modern users often feel overwhelmed by cluttered apps and complex interfaces.
There is a need for a **calm, simple, and structured platform** that helps users focus on mental wellness without distractions.

---

## 💡 The Solution

CampsCalm provides a **minimal, soothing, and intuitive interface** that helps users explore mindfulness content effortlessly.
The design prioritizes **clarity, accessibility, and emotional comfort**.

---

## 🎯 Design Goals

* Create a **calm and distraction-free UI**
* Ensure **easy navigation and readability**
* Build a **consistent design system**
* Design for **all screen sizes (responsive)**

## 🔍 Research Insights

* Too much information causes cognitive overload
* Calm visuals improve engagement and retention
* Clear hierarchy = better usability
